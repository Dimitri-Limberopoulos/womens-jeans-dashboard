================================================================================
DATA CLEANING APPLIED
================================================================================

1. PRICES (Current & Original)
   Format: Parsed "$31.99" → 31.99 (numeric)
   Both Current Price and Original Price fields normalized

2. FIT PARSING
   Split "Straight Leg with a Regular Fit" into:
   - leg_shape: Straight, Skinny, Slim, Wide, Flare, Bootcut, Crop, Capri, Ankle, Mom, Boyfriend
   - fit_style: Regular, Slim, Stretch, Relaxed

3. RISE STANDARDIZATION
   Merged values:
   - "Classic" + "Regular" → "Standard Rise"
   - "Ultra-High Rise" + "Super High Rise" → "Ultra-High Rise"
   Distribution: High Rise (1,693), Standard Rise (516), Other (390), Low Rise (61), Ultra-High Rise (10)

4. WASH CATEGORIZATION
   500+ color values mapped to 6 categories:
   - Black: 333 items (contains "black")
   - Dark Wash: 472 items (contains "dark", "indigo", "rinse")
   - Medium Wash: 270 items (contains "medium", "stonewash")
   - Light Wash: 266 items (contains "light", "bleach", "faded")
   - White: 154 items (contains "white", "cream", "ivory")
   - Color: 1,025 items (everything else)
   - Other: 153 items

5. INSEAM LENGTH
   Format: "30 Inches" → 30 (numeric)
   Nulls: "No Inseam Measurement" → null

6. COTTON PERCENTAGE
   Primary: Use % Cotton field if populated
   Fallback: Extract from Material field (e.g., "100% Cotton", "65% Cotton")
   Strategy: Prioritize explicit values, then parse Material field
